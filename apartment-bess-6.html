🔬 Molecule Systems — DividendVPP Analysis
Molecule doesn't create a new revenue stream — it sits between the utility VPP dispatch signal and your battery portfolio, guaranteeing each dispatch event is executed and telemetry-verified, then runs cross-program optimization (Lightsmith) so each site is always in the best-paying program available that hour. Most VPP portfolios without execution software realize ~55% of modeled value due to missed events and static enrollment. Molecule targets 95% realized capture. Revenue share: 70% asset owner / 20% Molecule / 10% installer (NextNRG captures installer 10% as installer of record).
