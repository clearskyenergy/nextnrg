HOW IT WORKS — 5 SIMPLE STEPS
From Quote to Power in 5 Steps
NextNRG manages the entire process — you just show up for commissioning.
1
Step One · Day 1
Share Your Utility Bill
Provide your last 12 months of utility bills. NextNRG analyzes your peak demand patterns, time-of-use charges, and demand spikes to size the right battery system for maximum savings — no site visit required at this stage.
📄 Upload PDF or photo of utility bill
2
Step Two · Week 1–2
Site Map & System Design
Our team conducts a brief site walk or works from your address to produce a site map showing the proposed BESS location, equipment footprint, conduit routing, and interconnection point. You review and approve the layout before any engineering begins.
🗺 Site map produced — like the one on page 2
3
Step Three · Week 2–6
Sign Agreement & Process Financing
Sign your site host or financing agreement. NextNRG submits interconnection applications to your utility, files for available rebates (ComEd BESH, SGIP, NJ BPU, etc.), and applies for applicable federal incentives including the 30% Investment Tax Credit.
✅ No upfront payment required
4
Step Four · Week 6–14
Installation & Commissioning
Licensed electricians and ClearSky's EPC team install the battery system, perform utility interconnection, and commission the system. We handle all inspections and AHJ approvals. Typical installation is 2–4 days with minimal disruption to your operations.
⚡ System goes live — savings begin immediately
5
Step Five · Ongoing
Sit Back & Save
ClearSky's EMS software monitors and optimizes your battery 24/7, dispatching it to shave demand peaks, participate in energy markets, and maximize your savings. You receive a monthly statement showing energy savings and any revenue share payments.
📊 Monthly savings reports delivered to your inbox
